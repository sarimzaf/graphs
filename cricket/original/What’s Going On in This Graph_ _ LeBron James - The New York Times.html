What’s Going On in This Graph? | LeBron James

This week, LeBron James, the Los Angeles Lakers forward, became the N.B.A.’s career scoring leader. Look at the graph: What do you notice?

By The Learning Network

Published Feb. 9, 2023Updated March 9, 2023

No one thought LeBron James would overtake Kareem Abdul-Jabbar to become the N.B.A.’s career scoring leader when he came to the league as an 18-year-old. It didn’t seem like anyone could.

The above graph was published by The New York Times the day that James broke Abdul-Jabbar’s scoring record.

On Wednesday, Feb. 15, we will moderate your responses live online. By Friday morning, Feb. 17, we will provide the “Reveal” — the graph’s free online link, additional questions, shout outs for student headlines and Stat Nuggets.
